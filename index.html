Bridge

Connecting neighbors through everyday help.

What is Bridge?

Bridge is a mobile-first platform that empowers neighbors to connect through small acts of assistance — like lending a hand with groceries, giving a ride, or walking a dog. Whether you need help or want to offer it, Bridge makes it simple, secure, and local.

How It Works

  • Create an account or log in with Google or Facebook
  • Request help or browse nearby requests
  • Connect through verified profiles
  • Earn karma points and community badges

Why Bridge?

Unlike broad social networks or gig apps, Bridge focuses on **hyperlocal**, **trust-based** help between people who live near each other. Think of it as the digital front porch for your neighborhood.
